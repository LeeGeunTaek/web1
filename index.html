WEB

The World Wide Web (WWW, W3) is a worldwide information space where people can share information through computers connected to the Internet . Often referred to simply as the Web . The term is often used synonymously with the Internet, but is strictly different. The web is just one service running on the Internet, like e-mail . However, since 1993, the web has been the absolute location of the Internet structure. In the Internet , it is a transmission method that exchanges pictures and characters using HTTP protocol , hypertext , and HTML format.
